Our Story

在多變的市場中

In A Shifting Market

芯浪國際有限公司 (CHIPWAVE GLOBAL LIMITED) 成立於 2021 年，總部坐落於全球貿易樞紐——香港。我們誕生的初衷很簡單：在波詭雲譎的半導體市場中，為客戶提供一個值得絕對信賴的供應支點。 Founded in 2021 and headquartered in the global trade hub of Hong Kong, Chipwave Global Limited was born from a simple mission: to provide clients with an absolute pivot of trust in the volatile semiconductor market.

我們不只是一家分銷商。我們將「敏捷採購」與「循環經濟」相結合，協助企業解決從芯片短缺到數據中心資產處置（ITAD）的全生命週期難題。透過全球化的網絡與嚴苛的質量檢測，我們確保每一顆芯片、每一台設備都能發揮最大價值。 We are more than a distributor. By combining "Agile Sourcing" with the "Circular Economy," we help enterprises solve lifecycle challenges ranging from chip shortages to Data Center IT Asset Disposition (ITAD).
